Welcome and thanks for stopping by…

Many years ago I turned my passion for crafting into a small business, to share my love of crochet and sewing with others. I can’t imagine sitting down at home in the evening without picking up my hooks to crochet a blanket or two. This simple hobby brings me lots of joy. I always carry odd balls of wool and my hooks to events, as I am often asked how to start a blanket, and of course I am only too happy to oblige. My lovely Mum, Pat, is an avid knitter and all my beautiful cardigans are down to her.

My stock is always changing and no two items are ever really the same. Check out my Gallery page for a selection of my creations. I am always trying out new ideas and designs.

I can put together a baby hamper full of handmade baby gifts. Each can be individually created to your requirements. Send me an email and an idea of what you would like and I will reply with a selection of photos of my current stock and we can take it from there. Or better still come and visit me at my next event.

I would love to meet you……

Amanda
