Courtesy of my 3 grown up sons, who took me to a Build-a-Bear Workshop for Mothers Day. I became the proud owner of Daisy.

She has been on a number of adventures around the country with my husband and I already. My friends (who think I'm ever so slightly potty) can't wait for the latest photos and updates.

It was on one of our trips that I realised there were many lonely teddy bears waiting for their forever home and big adventure. All my rescued bears have been lovingly washed, groomed and dressed.

And so my quest begins...

It would be lovely to see my rescued bears in their new home. please feel free to post a photo to my Facebook page.
